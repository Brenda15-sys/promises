About Counseling Psychology

Counseling psychology is a specialized field within psychology that focuses on providing therapeutic services to individuals, couples, families, and groups to address a wide range of psychological, emotional, and behavioral challenges.

Goal of Counseling Psychology: The primary goal is to help individuals improve their overall well-being and mental health by addressing issues such as anxiety, depression, stress, relationships, self-esteem, career transitions, and more.

Therapeutic Approaches: Counselors use various therapeutic approaches and techniques tailored to clients' needs, including cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based therapy, psychodynamic therapy, and more.
